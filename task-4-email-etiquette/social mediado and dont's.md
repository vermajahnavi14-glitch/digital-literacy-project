✅ DO’s of Social Media
1. Protect Your Privacy
Keep your accounts private when possible
Avoid sharing personal details like phone number, address, passwords
2. Think Before You Post
Ask yourself: Is this appropriate? Will it hurt someone?
Remember: once posted, it can stay online forever
3. Use Strong Passwords
Use a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols
Enable two-factor authentication
4. Be Respectful and Kind
Treat others the way you want to be treated
Avoid arguments, hate speech, or trolling
5. Verify Information Before Sharing
Check if news or information is true
Avoid spreading fake news
6. Manage Your Time
Set limits for social media usage
Focus on studies and real-life activities
7. Report Suspicious Activity
Report fake accounts, scams, or cyberbullying
Block users who make you uncomfortable


❌ DON’Ts of Social Media

1. Don’t Share Sensitive Information
Never share passwords, OTPs, or banking details
2. Don’t Accept Requests from Strangers
Be careful with unknown people online
Fake profiles are common
3. Don’t Post in Anger
Avoid posting when emotional or upset
It can lead to regret later
4. Don’t Spread Hate or Bully Others
Cyberbullying can seriously harm others
It can also have legal consequences
5. Don’t Believe Everything You See
Not all posts are real or trustworthy
Be critical of content
6. Don’t Overuse Social Media
Excessive use can affect mental health and productivity
7. Don’t Click on Suspicious Links
They may contain viruses or scams
Avoid unknown messages and offers
