📌 Project Overview

This project has been developed as a part of the CSE0001 – Digital Literacy course. In today’s fast-paced digital world, possessing digital skills is no longer optional but essential. As a Student Digital Ambassador, the primary objective of this project is to educate and empower peers with the knowledge required to use digital platforms responsibly, safely, and effectively.

The project focuses on enhancing awareness regarding:

Digital literacy and its importance
Safe and responsible internet usage
Professional communication in the digital space
Building a strong and credible digital identity

The project is structured into five comprehensive tasks, each addressing a specific dimension of digital literacy and practical application.

🎯 Objectives of the Project

The key objectives of this project are:

To promote awareness about digital literacy among students
To develop an understanding of online safety and cyber hygiene
To encourage responsible use of social media platforms
To build professional communication skills, especially email etiquette
To introduce students to coding and collaboration platforms
To spread awareness about cybercrime and preventive measures

📝 Detailed Task Description
🔹 Task 1: Digital Literacy Infographic

In this task, a visually appealing one-page infographic was created using Canva. The purpose of the infographic is to simplify and present key concepts of digital literacy in an easy-to-understand format.

Key highlights include:

Definition and importance of digital literacy
Safe internet browsing practices
Awareness about digital footprints
Useful digital tools for students

This task enhances creativity and helps in communicating complex ideas visually.

🔹 Task 2: Student Digital Portfolio

This task focuses on building a professional digital identity by creating profiles on important platforms such as:

GitHub (for showcasing coding projects)
LinkedIn (for professional networking)
Kaggle (for data science and AI learning)

Outcome:

Improved online presence
Better career opportunities
Development of a professional image
🔹 Task 3: Coding & Collaboration Platforms

In this task:

A beginner-level coding challenge was completed on HackerRank
A Google Form quiz was created to test and spread awareness about digital literacy

Skills Developed:

Basic programming and problem-solving
Understanding of collaborative tools
Ability to create and share interactive content
🔹 Task 4: Email Etiquette & Social Media Guide

This task emphasizes the importance of professional communication in digital environments.

Work Done:

Drafted formal and professional emails
Created a checklist of Do’s and Don’ts for social media usage

Focus Areas:

Writing clear and concise emails
Maintaining professionalism online
Avoiding misuse of social platforms
🔹 Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness

This task focuses on raising awareness about cyber threats and online safety.

Components:

A detailed case study on cybercrime
A prevention checklist to avoid digital fraud and threats

Topics Covered:

Types of cybercrime (phishing, identity theft, UPI fraud, etc.)
Real-life examples
Safety measures and precautions
