Prevention Tips While Using Social Media (Pointers)
------------------------------------------------------

Use strong and unique passwords

Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)

Keep your account private

Check and update privacy settings regularly

Do not share personal information (phone number, address, OTP, bank details)

Accept friend requests only from known people

Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links

Do not download files from untrusted sources

Be careful of online scams and fake offers

Do not share your live location publicly

Think before posting anything online

Block and report suspicious or abusive users

Log out from shared or public devices

Avoid saving passwords on public devices

Limit your social media usage time
